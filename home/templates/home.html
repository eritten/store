Nivee Company Limited.

We are a registered Limited Liability Company in the state of Delaware with a global reach. We pride ourself on the premises of saving our clients time and money.

our services

Bookkeeping

We provide daily, monthly, quarterly and annual bookkeeping services to small and medium size firms in North America and Europe.

Taxation

We provide individual and corporate tax solutions in North America. We also prepare individual tax returns for citizens living abroad.

Store Buffet

We own and manage this ecommerce platform for all kinds of shoppers who are looking to save time and money. Our platform makes it easy for shoppers to virtually search across all retail websites and compare prices, products, shipping, etc. Retailers on the other hand, are benefiting from less returns from customers as the app enables buyers to make an informed decisions prior to buying. Also, buyers can easily monitor rollbacks and new deals.

Software Development

We develop and market software for individuals and companies who are looking to bring efficiency to their lives and businesses. Our team of engineers are subject matter experts in over 32 programming languages.
